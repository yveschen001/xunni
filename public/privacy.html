Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 18, 2025 | Version 2.1

PRIVACY COMMITMENT
XunNi ("we", "us", or "Service") respects your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your data in compliance with international standards (including GDPR principles where applicable). This English version shall prevail over any translations.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Registration Data: Telegram User ID, Username, Display Name.
  • Profile Data: Nickname, Gender, Date of Birth, Time of Birth, Place of Birth.
  • Psychometric Data: MBTI test results, self-reported interests.

Note on Birth Data: We collect precise birth time and location solely for the purpose of generating astrological charts. This is a core functionality of the Service.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Interaction logs (bottles thrown/caught), feature usage statistics.
  • Technical Data: Device settings, language preference (via Telegram API).

2. Legal Basis & Purpose of Processing

We process your data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Performance of Contract: To provide the fortune-telling, matching, and anonymous chat services you requested.
  • Legitimate Interest: To maintain platform security, prevent fraud, and enforce our Terms of Service (e.g., age verification, anti-spam).
  • Consent: For optional features where you explicitly provide data (e.g., survey responses).

3. Data Usage & Matching Privacy

3.1 Social Matching

The Service allows users to generate compatibility reports. In this process:

PRIVACY BY DESIGN: Data Blindness
Your raw personal data (Birth Date/Time/Location) is processed server-side only. We NEVER disclose this raw data to other users during the matching process. Other users only receive the derived compatibility insights (e.g., "High Compatibility").

4. Data Sharing & International Transfers

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Service Providers: Cloudflare (Infrastructure/DB), OpenAI/Google (Translation/AI Processing - Anonymized where possible).
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law or to protect rights and safety.

International Transfer: Your data is stored in Cloudflare's distributed database (D1), which may replicate data across multiple jurisdictions to ensure low latency and high availability.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

5.1 Retention Period

We retain personal data as long as your account is active. Chat logs and temporary data may be auto-deleted after a set period (e.g., 90 days).

5.2 Account Deletion ("Right to be Forgotten")

You may request deletion via /delete_me. Upon confirmation:

  • Deleted: All personal profile data, chat history, and social graph data.
  • Retained (Legitimate Interest):
    • Financial Records: Transaction history (retained for tax/audit purposes).
    • Security Blocklist: Your Telegram ID hash (retained permanently to enforce the ban on re-registration and prevent ban evasion).

6. Security Measures

We employ industry-standard security measures including HTTPS/TLS encryption for data in transit and strict access controls for our databases. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is strictly for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we discover such data, we will delete it immediately.
